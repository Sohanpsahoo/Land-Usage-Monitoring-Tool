About Our Project

Learn more about our land classification AI model and its impact

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop advanced AI solutions for environmental monitoring and land use classification. By leveraging satellite imagery and machine learning, we aim to provide accurate and efficient tools for land cover analysis.

This project focuses on creating a robust model that can classify different types of land cover from satellite images, helping researchers, environmentalists, and policymakers make informed decisions about land use and conservation.

Our Approach

We use a combination of deep learning techniques and satellite imagery to create our land classification model. Our approach involves:

  • Collecting high-quality satellite imagery data
  • Preprocessing and augmenting the data
  • Training a CNN model on the processed data
  • Evaluating and fine-tuning the model
  • Deploying the model for real-world applications
