Current Applications

Urban Planning

Assists city planners in making informed decisions about urban development by identifying land use patterns and monitoring urban sprawl. Helps optimize infrastructure placement and green space allocation.

Environmental Conservation

Monitors deforestation, tracks changes in vegetation cover, and identifies areas requiring conservation efforts. Supports biodiversity preservation and ecosystem management.

Agriculture Management

Helps farmers and agricultural organizations optimize land use, monitor crop health, and plan irrigation systems. Supports precision agriculture and sustainable farming practices.

Water Resource Management

Identifies water bodies, monitors changes in water levels, and helps manage watersheds. Essential for flood prediction and water conservation efforts.

Industrial Development

Assists in site selection for industrial facilities, ensuring minimal environmental impact and optimal resource accessibility. Supports sustainable industrial growth.

Change Detection

Monitors and analyzes land use changes over time, providing valuable data for policy makers and environmental researchers. Tracks urbanization and natural habitat changes.
